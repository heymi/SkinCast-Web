We are excited to introduce SkinCast, a new way to understand how today's weather affects your skin.

### What SkinCast Does

SkinCast uses AI to read real-time climate data — humidity, temperature, UV index, and environmental stress — and translates it into clear, personalized skin guidance you can act on in seconds.

### Key Features

- **Daily Skin Score** — See an at-a-glance score that highlights overall skin stress, surfaces the top risk, and breaks down the conditions shaping how your skin may feel today.
- **Personalized Concerns** — Set your skin concerns once (acne, dryness, sensitivity, redness, and more), and every daily read adapts to what matters most to you.
- **AI Climate Analysis** — Understand how humidity, temperature, UV, and climate stress work together to affect your skin, with plain-language explanations.
- **Action Plans** — Get a focused care plan for the day, from hydration support to barrier recovery, based on current conditions and your skin profile.
- **Home Screen Widgets** — Check today's skin score, top risk, and care focus right from your Home Screen.
- **Pro Insights** — Unlock richer analysis, clearer explanations, and more personalized guidance with the Pro tier.

### Private by Design

SkinCast works without registration or login. No account, no sign-in, no tracking. Your skin data stays on your device.
